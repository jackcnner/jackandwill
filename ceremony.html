All the Details

Together with their parents, Jack Conner and William Channon request the pleasure of your company at their wedding.

A civil ceremony is to be held at the Bristol Register Office at the Old Council House, Corn Street, Bristol. The service will be followed by a light lunch at San Carlo, Corn Street, and a wedding reception in the evening at M Shed, Wapping Road.


Location


The ceremony will be held at the Bristol Register Office at the Old Council House, Corn Street, Bristol.

The register office is located at the top of Corn Street, near to the Harbour Hotel and St. Nicholas Market.

The register office is a twenty minute walk from Bristol Temple Meads station, or is a short walk from all buses serving Union Street or Wine Street.

Access by road is from the M32. There are multiple car parks available in the city centre, although the closest is The Galleries shopping mall car park. Please be aware that the register office is located within the Bristol clean air zone and charges may apply.

On arrival, please enter through the doors at the front of the building (not the office entrance at the side!), and head for the Mayoral Room.
