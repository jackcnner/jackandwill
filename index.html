All the Details

Please join us for an evening of dinner and dancing

If you have any dietary requirements, please indicate this when you RSVP.


Location


The reception will be held in the Event Suite at M Shed, Princes Wharf, Wapping Road, Bristol, BS1 4RN.

The M Shed is a twenty minute walk from Bristol Temple Meads station, or is a short walk from all buses serving Broad Quay.

Access by road is from the M32. There is no on-site parking, however the nearest car parks are The Grove or Wapping Wharf, both a short walk to the venue.

On arrival at the venue, please head to the Event Suite on the Second Floor. Lifts are available.
Timings


The reception will begin at 07:00PM.

Welcome drinks will be served on arrival to the venue, followed by a buffet at 07:30PM.

Live music and dancing will begin at 08:30PM.

Due to licensing restrictions, guests are asked kindly asked to leave the venue by 01:00.
Dining


A seasonal buffet, with a variety of meat, fish, and vegetarian options, will be served, with further late night snacks for those wishing to indulge themselves!

If you have any dietary requirements, please indicate so on your RSVP and our caterers will prepare a specially curated menu for you.
Accomodation


There are a variety of hotels, bed and breakfasts, and rentals available within the Bristol city centre and surrounding.

The Bristol Hotel

ibis Bristol Centre

Please let us know if you need any assistance in finding suitable accomodation.
